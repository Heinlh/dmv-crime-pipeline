Email Alerts

One email per day with the same plain-English brief as the Daily Brief page: what happened, where, and how it compares to a typical day.

Sign up

Email alerts aren't live yet. Site owner setup: create a free account at Buttondown, set BUTTONDOWN_USERNAME in site/js/common.js, and add a BUTTONDOWN_API_KEY repository secret so the daily pipeline can send the digest.

Signups are handled by Buttondown with a confirmation email (double opt-in). Your address is stored by Buttondown, never by this site or its code repository, and is used only to send the daily brief. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. See the privacy policy.

What you'll get

  • One email per day, sent after the morning data refresh.
  • Bullet-point summary of the latest data day: totals vs. a typical day, top category, per-jurisdiction counts.
  • The day's most serious incidents, each with its official agency label and block-level location.
  • Links back to the live map and trends for the full picture.

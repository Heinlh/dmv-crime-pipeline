Who I am

Hi, I'm Hein Linn Htet, a DMV resident. I built DMV Crime Watch for three reasons: to make something genuinely useful for the community I live in, where public safety data is published by three different agencies in three different formats and nobody should need to be a data engineer to read it; to get hands-on experience building a real, end-to-end data product, from API extraction and warehouse modeling to visualization and a daily email; and honestly, because it was fun to build.

Everything here is open source, runs on free infrastructure, and updates itself every morning. If you spot something wrong, have an idea, or want to talk data engineering, I'd love to hear from you.

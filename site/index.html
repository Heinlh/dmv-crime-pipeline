    What am I looking at?

    • Use the search box on the map to jump to a crime type or a place (a ward, district, or city); suggestions come from the loaded incidents, so nothing you type leaves your browser.
    • Hover a dot to see the offense; click it for the full summary card.
    • Numbers in circles show how many incidents are grouped together at the current zoom level. Zoom in to split them apart.
    • Only incidents with a published location are shown; locations are block-level as published, never sharpened.
    • Hotspot shading marks hex cells (about 0.7 km²) where incidents concentrated over the selected window; brighter means more incidents. It shows where reports cluster, it does not predict anything.
    • "Min. severity" uses this project's own 1-10 weighting (10 = homicide), not an official police scale.
    Data as of last published agency data, not live. Sources: dataMontgomery, DC MPD Crime Incidents, OpenPGC, FCPD, and PWC Police public crime services. Looking for older data? Trends covers the full history since 2016. See About for how this works. · Privacy policy
